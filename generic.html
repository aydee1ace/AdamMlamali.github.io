Motor Insurance Claims
Prediction Project

Introduction

The dataset used for this project was sourced from: Kaggle - Car Insurance Claim Data .

The data includes around 8,000 records of car insurance policyholders. It features various attributes such as age, income, gender, and claim history. Two target variables are included:

  • Whether a claim has occurred (classification).
  • The total cost of the claim, if a claim has been made (regression).

Objective

The project is divided into two parts:

  1. Classification: Predicting whether a claim will occur.
  2. Regression: Predicting the value of a claim, assuming one has occurred.

Results

The performance of the regression model was as follows:

  • RMSE: 8383.78
  • MAE: 3515.91
We can see that the RMSE was slightly higher than what was observed during training, but still relatively in line. There is room for potential improvement, such as:
  • Advanced feature engineering
  • Exploring feature importance
  • Building ensemble models
